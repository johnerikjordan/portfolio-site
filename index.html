About Me

I'm a UX Design Leader with over a decade of experience building teams, scaling systems, and shaping strategy across B2B SaaS, EdTech, and enterprise platforms. I bridge strategic leadership with hands-on execution—combining 7+ years of team management with deep IC design skills.

My leadership journey began in 2015 at Babbel, where I established the foundation for team development practices I still apply today: structured career paths, regular 1:1s, and feedback-driven cultures. Since then, I've scaled Babbel Magazine to millions of readers, led enterprise-scale design systems at STRATO AG, and built Smart Agency's first UX/UI team from scratch. Each role has sharpened my ability to balance strategic direction with the willingness to roll up my sleeves and design when needed.

I'm particularly energized by challenges that require both diplomatic leadership and operational excellence—evolving design systems, improving cross-functional processes, and fostering environments where designers grow. My approach balances people development with product outcomes: I mentor designers, facilitate discovery workshops, and champion user-centered practices that align with business goals.

Currently based in Berlin, I'm seeking leadership or senior design roles in organizations focused on education, climate, or public good – places where my strategic design initiatives can create meaningful impact.

And outside of work? I’m originally from a suburb of L.A. named after Tarzan (no joke). I love philosophical sci-fi, tabletop RPGs, and following my hometown baseball team, the L.A. Dodgers. I’m a pretty decent DIY handyman, and I’m trying to expand that skillset into gardening (with mixed results). Lately I've been playing around with LLMs, building agentic helpers, and experimenting with AI-augmented workflows. It’s a strange new world, but also a fun playground. When I’m not doing any of the above, I’m either building Lego or drawing with my son.
